About This Application

The Quality Indicator (QI) portal will allow the I-SPY users to submit and retrieve tissue core quality data. For the I-SPY study, two frozen and two formalin-fixed biopsy specimens are collected prior to the start of chemotherapy, 2-4 days after the start of chemotherapy, between chemotherapeutic regimens (if applicable), and at the end of chemotherapy.
